# DENTITION & ORAL ANATOMY
### Course Materials & Complete Resource Guide | Prepared by: Sarah Abumandil

Everything you need for the Dentition course is right here. This repository has slides, textbooks, audio notes, and past exams collected from different universities (Cairo University, Al-Zaytuna, FUE) to help you study easily and ace your exams.

---

## Repository Navigation

### 1. [01-Introduction-and-Core-Textbooks](./01-Introduction-and-Core-Textbooks/)
* Official college textbook and comprehensive initial summaries.
* Covers dental numbering systems, mandible anatomy, and landmarks.
* Includes all foundational audio explanations for Chapter 1.

### 2. [02-Permanent-Anterior-Teeth](./02-Permanent-Anterior-Teeth/)
* Slides, summaries, and handouts for permanent front teeth.
* Detailed morphology for both Maxillary and Mandibular Incisors and Canines.

### 3. [03-Permanent-Posterior-Teeth](./03-Permanent-Posterior-Teeth/)
* Slides, structural diagrams, and clinical notes for back teeth.
* Comprehensive coverage of Maxillary and Mandibular Premolars and Molars.

### 4. [04-Deciduous-Dentition](./04-Deciduous-Dentition/)
* Everything about baby teeth (primary dentition) development and dimensions.
* Critical comparative charts between permanent and deciduous teeth.

### 5. [05-Occlusion-Advanced-Topics-and-Diagrams](./05-Occlusion-Advanced-Topics-and-Diagrams/)
* Occlusion lectures, compensatory curves, and tooth eruption timelines.
* High-quality pulp cavity diagrams, cross-sections, and dental posters.
* Introductory lectures to other dental specialties (Pedodontics, Prosthodontics, Surgery, Periodontics).

### 6. [06-Questions-Banks-and-Exams](./06-Questions-Banks-and-Exams/)
* **01-Question-Banks-and-Summaries:** Main compilation files and quick MCQ helpers.
* **02-University-Exams-and-Quizzes:** General university quizzes, midterms, and test sheets.
* **03-Qasr-El-Eyni-Past-Exams:** Cairo University / Qasr El-Eyni official past exams (2015-2020).
* **04-DDS-Comprehensive-Finals:** Full dentistry board finals and DDS comprehensive exams.
